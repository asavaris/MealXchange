MealXchange

Simple | Efficient | Secure | Transparent

At Princeton, the current pen-and-paper system for monitoring meal exchanges between eating clubs is inconvenient for everyone involved. Members don't know how many meal exchanges they have outstanding with their friends and club administrators spend hours manually tabulating meal exchanges at the end of each month. We're proposing a system where instead of using paper slips, students simply register their meal exchanges and guest meals online on our webapp. Students can receive updates on how many meal exchanges they have outstanding as well as how many guest meals they have left in the month. Eating clubs can edit memberships and club preferences, as well as view all exchanges online or by downloading a .csv file with all the computations done for them. Fewer lost slips of paper, fewer outstanding meal exchanges, fewer hours of manual tabulation. Everyone's a winner!

Register a Meal Exchange

To register a meal exchange login to your club account and select meal exchange from the options provided on the home page.


This will redirect you to a page that looks like this. Enter the netID of the member under Host netID and the netID of the person they would like to meal exchange with under Guest netID.


If the meal exchange was successful you will be sent to the following thank you page. Both the member and the guest will recieve a confirmation link in an email from princeton.mealxchange@gmail.com. For the meal exchange to be recorded both parties must click through the link to verify the exchange.


Register a Guest Meal

To register a guest meal login to your club account and select guest meal from the options provided on the home page.


This will redirect you to a page that looks like this. Enter the netID of the member under Host netID.


If you have sufficient guest meals left you will be sent to the following thank you page. If not you will be sent to an error page. The member will recieve a confirmation link in an email from princeton.mealxchange@gmail.com. For the guest meal to be recorded the host must click through the link to verify the guest meal.

View Completed Exchanges

To view completed exchanges press the hamburger button on the right hand side and select View Exchanges.


This will take you to a page that looks like this. You can sort the exchanges by clicking on any of the blue table headers. You can also search for all exchanges for a given member by entering their netID in the search field in the upper left corner.


To download all exchanges from this month and last month click Download .csv. This will output an excel file with a list of your members and all the completed meal exchanges.


View Incomplete Exchanges


To view incomplete exchanges press the hamburger button on the right hand side and select View Exchanges.


This will take you to a page that looks like this. Click on View Unconfirmed exchanges.



You will be redirected to the following page. Just like the View Exchanges page you can sort by any of the table headers and search for a specific member's unconfirmed exchanges using the search field in the upper left corner. You can also download a .csv with the confirmed exchanges and a list of members. To go back to View Exchanges click on the right-most button View Exchanges.

Manage Your Club Preferences

If you have not yet set your club preferences you will automatically be redirected to do so. In club preferences you set your meal hours and the number of guest meals each member can have. This allows us to prevent members from trying to meal exchange outside of meal hours and stop members from using more guests than they are alotted. If you would like to edit your preferences click on the hamburger button and select Club Preferences.


This will take you to a page that looks like this. You can reset your preferences whenever you like provided you are logged into your account. Make sure to hit save before leaving this page.

Edit Your Membership

To add or remove members click on the hamburger button and select Edit Membership.


This will take you to a page that looks like this. You can delete members by either individually selecting the checkboxes next to their name and pressing Delete or by searching for a common attribute, i.e. 2016, and pressing Delete All. To add members click Add Members.


To add members click Add Members. This will redirect you to the following page. We allow you to add members by class year. To do this ;ist all the names, first and last , in the top field and the corresponding netIDs in the bottom field. Make sure these are in the same order. Then select their class year and click Submit.
